Feta Pasta Salad

1                   red bell pepper, 1/2 inch strips
1                   yellow bell pepper, chopped
1                   eggplant, cubed
3                   small yellow squash, 1/4 inch slices
1 1/2 oz            sundried tomatoes, soaked in 1/2 cup boiling water
1/2 cup             torn arugula leaves
1/2 cup             fresh basil
2 tbsp              balsamic vinegar
2 tbsp              minced garlic
4 oz                crumbled feta cheese
6 tbsp              virgin olive oil
12 oz               farfalle pasta
1/4 tsp             salt
1/4 tsp             ground black pepper

Oven 450 F

Cookie sheet with tin foil.  Spray with nonstick.

Combine vegetables, 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper.  Toss with
hands.  Bake for 25 minutes.  Toss occassionally and brown.

In large pot, cook pasta and drain.

Drain softened tomatoes, reserve water.

Combine pasta, arugula, basil, tomatoes, and vegetables.  Add oil,
water, garlic, feta, and vinegar.  Toss to coat.

Season with salt and pepper.  Refrigerate.
